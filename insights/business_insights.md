# Business Insights & Recommendations

## Customer Satisfaction & Delivery

- Customers experiencing 7+ day delivery delays give significantly lower review scores (1.70 avg) compared to on-time deliveries (4.29 avg).
- Delivery delays beyond 5 days reduce customer satisfaction by approximately 2.6 review points.
- Improving delivery performance in AL and MA states should be prioritized due to the highest late delivery percentages.

## Customer Retention

- 35.2% of customers fall into the At-Risk segment.
- Recoverable revenue from At-Risk customers is estimated at approximately R$849K.
- Re-engagement campaigns and loyalty incentives could help reduce customer churn.

## Seller Performance

- Top 10% sellers contribute 66.8% of total platform revenue.
- Protecting and incentivizing high-performing sellers is critical for platform stability.
- Revenue concentration indicates potential dependency risk on a small seller group.

## Regional Operations

- SP contributes 37.1% of total platform revenue.
- Northeast states show significantly higher late delivery rates compared to SP.
- Logistics optimization opportunities exist in underperforming regions.

## Payment Behavior

- Credit cards account for 73.9% of transactions and 78.3% of revenue.
- Installment buyers spend 105.2% more per order than non-installment buyers.
- Installment purchasing behavior strongly influences platform revenue generation.

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Improve delivery operations in high-delay regions.
2. Launch retention campaigns targeting At-Risk customers.
3. Strengthen partnerships with top-performing sellers.
4. Optimize logistics in Northeast states.
5. Leverage installment purchasing behavior through targeted promotions.
